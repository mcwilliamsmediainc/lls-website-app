Truskett Law: Personal Injury Attorneys Serving Tulsa and Northeast Oklahoma

A Tulsa personal injury practice built around injured people and their families.

When someone gets hurt because another person or company acted carelessly, the path forward can feel overwhelming. Medical bills arrive before you know what your case is worth. Insurance adjusters call before you have time to think. Truskett Law exists to stand between injured people and that pressure.

The firm is based in Tulsa, Oklahoma, and focuses entirely on personal injury law. That focus matters. Every case the firm accepts involves someone who was hurt, or a family that lost someone, because of another party’s negligence.

A Practice Built Around Injured People

John Truskett graduated law school in 2004 and has spent his legal career on the plaintiff side of personal injury cases [VERIFY total years in practice and firm founding date]. That is not a small distinction. Plaintiff-side personal injury work means the firm’s interests are directly tied to its clients’ outcomes. Truskett Law works on contingency, which means clients pay no attorney fees unless the firm recovers money for them.

That structure removes a barrier that stops many injured people from getting help. You do not need money to get started. You do not pay by the hour while your situation is still uncertain.

What the Firm Handles

Truskett Law takes cases across the full range of personal injury claims, with particular depth in motor vehicle accidents and nursing home negligence.

Motor vehicle and transportation cases:

  • Car and truck accidents
  • Semi-truck and tractor-trailer collisions
  • Motorcycle accidents
  • Pedestrian injuries
  • SUV rollover and ATV accidents

Premises and liability cases:

  • Slip, trip, and fall injuries
  • Animal and dog bite injuries
  • Defective products and product liability

Medical and institutional negligence:

  • Nursing home negligence and elderly abuse
  • Birth injuries
  • Surgical injuries and medical malpractice
  • Catastrophic injuries

Insurance and property claims:

  • Insurance disputes
  • Property damage claims

Wrongful death representation is also available for families who have lost someone due to another party’s negligence.

How the Firm Works

Truskett Law handles cases for people who are unrepresented and often dealing with an insurance company that has far more experience in these situations than they do. The firm’s job is to close that gap.

Consultations are how that process starts. There is no commitment required to have a conversation about your situation. The firm reviews what happened, explains what the case may involve, and gives you honest information about your options.
