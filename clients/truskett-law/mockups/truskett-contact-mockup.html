Request a free case review or reach the firm directly. There is no fee unless the firm recovers for you.

If you or someone in your family has been injured due to another person’s negligence, the right time to get answers is now. Use the form on this page to request a case review. A member of our team will follow up to learn about your situation, answer your initial questions, and explain what working with Truskett Law looks like from here.

There is no fee to speak with us, and you pay nothing unless we recover for you.

What to have ready: A brief description of what happened, the date of the incident, and any relevant paperwork you have on hand, such as an accident report, medical records, or insurance correspondence. Do not worry if you are still gathering information. We can work through the details together.
